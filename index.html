Animal Score

Rules! (click to expand)

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four animals below. By clicking on an animal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. The value of each aniaml is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each animal.
